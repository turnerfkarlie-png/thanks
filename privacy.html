Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Workforce Mobility & Employment Program collects and uses your information when you apply through our portal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details provided by you in the application form, including your name, email address, phone number, and professional background.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used solely to assess your eligibility for the Workforce Mobility & Employment Program and to contact you regarding your application status.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with program partners and recruiters specifically involved in the placement process for this program.

4. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.
